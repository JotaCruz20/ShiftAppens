About Dr.Music


Nowadays, with the pandemic we are facing, more and more people are feeling lonely and depressed, and this greatly affects their mental health. A Harvard Medical School study concluded that music therapy can help people by evoking emotional responses in order to relax, stimulate, or help them heal. In this way, music enables you to:
-Calm stress
-Reduce pain
-Improve cognitive performance
-Be a source of entertainment at the most exhausting moments
With this problem in mind, Dr. Music, through a questionnaire and an Artificial Intelligence algorithm, makes an evaluation of the user and creates a playlist on Spotify that will contain, depending on what the user prefers at the moment
